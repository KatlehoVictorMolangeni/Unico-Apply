Frequently Asked Questions

What is Unico-Apply? ▼

Unico-Apply is a platform designed to help South African Matric candidates apply to universities and colleges. We handle the entire application process on your behalf, making it easier and stress-free.

How does Unico-Apply work? ▼

Students register on myUnico platform, submit their details, an track their application status.

What documents do I need to apply through Unico-Apply? ▼

You will need your certified ID copy, Matric certificate or latest results, proof of address, and any additional documents required by the institution you are applying to.

Is there a fee to use Unico-Apply? ▼

Yes, Unico-Apply charges a small service fee to manage your applications. This fee applies to bursaries as well.

Can I track my application status on Unico-Apply? ▼

Yes, you can track your application status through your myUnico dashboard. You will receive regular updates and notifications about your application's progress.
