Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01- July- 2025

At Unico-Apply, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your data when you visit or use our services, including our website and associated applications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with a seamless and personalized experience. This includes:

1.1 Personal Information

When you register on Unico-Apply, apply for university or college programs, or communicate with us, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Home Address
  • Gender
  • Academic Qualifications
  • Matric Results

1.2 Account Information

We also collect information related to your user account, such as:

  • Username
  • Password
  • Application Status
  • Application History

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect usage data when you interact with our website, such as:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Operating System
  • Device Type
  • Pages Viewed
  • Time Spent on the Site
  • Interaction with Features

1.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies help us remember your preferences and improve the functionality of our site. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate your application process, including submitting and tracking applications to higher education institutions.
  • To communicate with you regarding your application status, updates, and notifications.
  • To improve our website's functionality and user experience based on your interaction with our platform.
  • To personalize the content and services offered to you.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send you important administrative notices, such as updates to our Privacy Policy or terms of service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With educational institutions: To process your applications and communicate with the universities or colleges you are applying to.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in our operations, such as website hosting, data analysis, and email services. These providers are obligated to use your information only for the services they provide.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information if required by law or in response to a legal request by public authorities, such as a court order or government investigation.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to sensitive data.

While we strive to protect your information, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

As a user of Unico-Apply, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: If you believe any information we have about you is inaccurate, you can request to update or correct it.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information, provided it is not necessary for us to retain it for legal or operational purposes.
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of receiving promotional or marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in any email you receive.
  • Data Portability: You may request to transfer your personal information to another service provider, where feasible.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [insert contact details].

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. These third-party sites are not under our control, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
